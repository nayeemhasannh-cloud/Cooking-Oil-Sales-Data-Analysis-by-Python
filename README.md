Supply Chain KPI Dashboard Excel Python

Project Overview
This project analyzes 24 months of FMCG supply chain data Cooking Oil 1L bottle to evaluate operational efficiency using key performance indicators KPIs. The goal is to identify bottlenecks in inventory management and supplier performance and support data driven decision making.

Dataset Summary
- Time Period Jan 2024 to Dec 2025 24 months
- Product Cooking Oil 1L bottle
- Observations 24 monthly records
- Variables Demand Inventory Lead Time Stock Received Stock out events

Key Performance Indicators KPIs

1 Inventory Turnover
- Average Inventory Turnover 3.94
- Range 2.71 to 6.25
- Insight Efficiency improves over time but volatility indicates inconsistent inventory utilization

2 Stock out Rate
- Overall Stock out Rate 25 percent
- Total Stock out Months 6 out of 24 months
- Insight One in four months experienced stock outs indicating service level risk

3 Lead Time
- Average Lead Time 6.58 days
- Maximum Lead Time 9 days
- Minimum Lead Time 5 days
- Insight Lead time variability contributes to stock out events during peak demand

4 Demand Analysis
- Total Demand 49660 units
- Average Monthly Demand 2069 units
- Peak Demand Month December 2025 2500 units
- Insight Strong seasonal upward trend in demand toward year end

5 Fill Rate
- Average Fill Rate 1.04
- Insight Slight over supply in several months indicating inefficient ordering patterns

Key Bottlenecks Identified

- Stock out concentration occurred during high demand months June July 2024 November December 2024 July 2025 December 2025
- Lead time instability fluctuated between 5 to 9 days impacting replenishment reliability
- Inventory imbalance overcorrection in supply during low demand months increased holding inefficiency

Business Recommendations

- Implement safety stock policy 15 to 20 percent to reduce stock out probability
- Improve supplier agreements to stabilize lead time within plus or minus 1 day variation
- Align procurement cycles with demand seasonality especially Q4 peak demand
- Optimize order quantities to reduce overstocking during low demand months

Tools Used
- Python Pandas Matplotlib
- Excel data structuring
- KPI analytics and time series evaluation

Outcome
This project demonstrates how supply chain KPIs can be used to
- Identify inefficiencies in inventory and procurement
- Quantify operational risk 25 percent stock out rate
- Support strategic inventory planning using data driven insights
